C Design Lab

Graduate Assistant for C Design Lab working under Dr. Karthik Ramani. I worked for the Gifted Education Resource Institute (GERI) project.

Roles and Achievements

• Designed 3D printed connectors, mechanical linkages and specific use case models for hand gesture-controlled robotics kit that enables users of any knowledge level to easily form and shape their ideas.
• Understand design methodology of novice designers using design-based learning principles with middle school children on Robot design.

• Developed analytic tools for analysis of big data such as videos, audios, questionnaires, and interviews.
• The data collected is then parsed into different stages or learning and designing such as Visual Learning, Tangible Planning, Verbal Planning and 13 other categories.
• This coded data provides rich information about where novice designers face problems during design, how and when they communicate with their peers, and observe what a successful design and learning pattern looks like.
